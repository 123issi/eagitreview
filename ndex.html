About Our School

East African Girls in Tech is dedicated to bridging the gender gap in technology across East Africa through quality education and mentorship.

Empowering the Next Generation of Female Tech Leaders

Founded in 2020, East African Girls in Tech has trained over 1,000 girls in various technology disciplines. Our mission is to create equal opportunities for girls in the fast-growing tech industry.

We partner with leading tech companies and certification bodies to provide our students with recognized credentials that open doors to rewarding careers.

Hands-on Learning

Our courses emphasize practical skills through real-world projects and labs.

Free Certifications

We cover the cost of all certification exams for our female students.

Career Support

We provide job placement assistance and internship opportunities.
